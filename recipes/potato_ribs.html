Potato Pork Ribs

Description

This dish features tender pork ribs cooked with potatoes, seasoned with soy sauce and spices.

It is a popular dish in East Asian cultures.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork ribs
  • 500 g potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, minced
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 green onions, chopped

Instructions

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and ginger, saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add pork ribs and cook until browned on all sides.
  4. Stir in soy sauce, sugar, sesame oil, and black pepper.
  5. Peel and cut potatoes into chunks, add to the pot.
  6. Pour in water, bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low.
  7. Cover and simmer for about 1 hour, or until the ribs are tender and potatoes are cooked through.
  8. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper if needed.
  9. Garnish with chopped green onions before serving.
  10. Serve hot with steamed rice or as a main dish.
