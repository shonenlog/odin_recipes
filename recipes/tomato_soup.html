Tomato Soup

Description

This dish features a rich and creamy tomato soup, perfect for a comforting meal.

It is a popular dish in many cultures, especially during colder months.

Ingredients

  • 4 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a pot over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions and garlic, saut√© until translucent.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook for about 10 minutes until soft.
  4. Pour in vegetable broth and add sugar, salt, and pepper.
  5. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, then blend until smooth.
  6. If desired, stir in heavy cream for a richer flavor.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves.
